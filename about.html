About BIT26

BIT26 is an innovative class designed to prepare students for the ever-evolving world of technology and web development.

Our mission is to provide hands-on, practical education that bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. We believe in learning by doing, which is why our curriculum is project-based and industry-focused.

Founded in 2023, BIT26 has quickly become known for producing graduates who are not just technically proficient, but also creative problem-solvers and effective communicators.

Our instructors are industry professionals who bring their expertise and experience directly to the classroom, ensuring that students learn the most current and relevant skills.

Join us at BIT26 and become part of a community that's passionate about technology, innovation, and growth.
